Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to share with the world what dinosaurs I think is cool and for people to respond and share their own opinions. While also fulfilling the assignment specifications.

Audience

The audience for this site will be those who are invested in dinosaurs and have strong opinions about them.

Dynamic elements

I will include a json with dinosaur page details that can be used to dynamically add more dinosaurs. A thumbnail of dinosaurs will also be included on the home page (generated from same json) which links to the main pages associated with each dinosaur. Also each dinosaur page will let users agree or disagree and a dynamic form will appear and ask for the users feedback. After form is completed a message will appear indicating the feedback was accepted or rejected.

Content

Home page

My name is Jaron Andrus. And as a child, dinosaurs were incredibly important to me. That was because dinosaurs are objectively cool animals. There’s something fascinating about creatures that once ruled the Earth—towering, scaly beasts that roamed jungles, plains, and oceans, only to vanish in a prehistoric cataclysm. The idea that these massive reptiles once existed feels almost mythical, yet we have the fossils to prove it. And while all dinosaurs are cool in their own way, some stand out more than others.

In this site, I want to highlight the dinosaurs that I think are the absolute coolest. Whether it’s their size, their speed, or just the sheer awe they inspire, these creatures deserve recognition. Of course, this list is entirely my opinion, but I’ve thought long and hard about it. Every dinosaur featured here has earned its spot for a reason, and I’m prepared to defend my choices.

But this isn't just about my opinions—it's about yours too. So please, click below to see my reasoning, and let me know what you think. Do you agree? Do you think I left out a truly legendary dinosaur? I want to hear from you! Share your thoughts, and let's celebrate the most incredible creatures to ever walk the Earth together.
